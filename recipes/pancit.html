Pancit

Description

This quick pancit recipe with chicken, vegetables, and rice noodles is easy and delicious. It will remind you of island fiestas!

Pancit is a Filipino dish. There are many different types of pancit, but it always features rice noodles and vegetables (and often meat, such as pork or chicken).

Ingredients

  • Dried Rice Noodle
  • Oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Chicken Breast
  • Cabbage
  • Carrot
  • Soy sauce
  • Lemon

Direction

  • Step 1

    • Soften dried noodles, drain and set aside

  • Step 2

    • Heat oil over medium-low heat. Add onion and Garlic; cook and stir until onion is tender, about 3 to 5 minutes.

  • Step 3

    • Stir in chicken, cabbage, carrots, and soysauce. Cook until cabbage begin to soften Toss in noodles

  • Step 4

    • Transfer pacit to a sering dish and garnish with lemon wedges
