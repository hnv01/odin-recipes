Ramen Noodles

Description

Ramen noodles, what is there to be said, everyone knows what ramen is, right?

Ingredients

  • Vegetable broth
  • Ramen noodles package
  • Soy sauce
  • Chill oil
  • Fresh ginger root
  • Sesame oil
  • Green onion

Direction

  • Step 1

    • Combine broth and noodles in a saucepan.

  • Step 2

    • Cover and bring to boil, stir and break up noodles

  • Step 3

    • Reduce heat then add soy sauce, chili oil and ginger.

  • Step 4

    • Simmer, uncovered for 10m.

  • Step 5

    • Stir in sesame oil and garnish with green oil.
